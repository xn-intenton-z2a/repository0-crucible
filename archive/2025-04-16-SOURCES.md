# ReAct Pattern (Reasoning and Acting)
## https://arxiv.org/abs/2110.07602
This research paper introduces the ReAct (Reasoning and Acting) pattern for large language models. It outlines how models can integrate reasoning with direct action-taking, which is highly relevant for agent-based automation and intelligent, self-improving systems. The paper is widely cited in the AI community.
## Public Domain (Research Paper)
